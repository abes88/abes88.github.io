Acupuncture

Acupuncture consists of a group of therapies, of which filiform, or fine needle, is the most well known and practiced one. Filiform needle treatment is conducted by using sterile disposable stainless needles to stimulate energy passway, called Jing Luo or Channels, and collaterals to activate the potential healing ability of the body and harmonize organs and systems. Acupuncture has been practiced for thousands of years in China, and is used to treat all kinds of diseases. Both clinical practice and modern studies have proven that acupuncture is a safe and effective treatment.
